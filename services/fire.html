Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fire NOC?+
A Fire NOC (No Objection Certificate) is a mandatory certificate issued by the fire department certifying that a building or establishment meets all fire safety requirements. It is required for commercial establishments, factories, hospitals, educational institutions, and other public buildings to ensure fire safety compliance.
Who needs a Fire NOC?+
Fire NOC is required for commercial establishments above a certain size, including offices, malls, hospitals, educational institutions, factories, hotels, restaurants, and other public buildings. The specific requirements vary by state and the type of establishment. Most buildings with a built-up area of 500 square meters or more require a Fire NOC.
What documents are required for Fire NOC?+
Documents typically required include building plans, fire fighting scheme drawings, occupancy certificate, property documents, identity proof of the owner, and other statutory clearances. The specific requirements vary by state and the type of establishment. Our team helps prepare all necessary documentation.
How long does it take to get a Fire NOC?+
The timeline for Fire NOC approval varies by state and the complexity of the project. Typically, it takes 30-60 days from application submission to approval, provided all documentation is complete and the establishment meets fire safety requirements. Our team ensures timely follow-up to minimize delays.
How often do Fire NOCs need to be renewed?+
Fire NOCs typically need to be renewed every 3-5 years, depending on the state regulations and the type of establishment. The exact timeline varies by state. Our team tracks all renewal deadlines and ensures timely filing to maintain continuous compliance.
